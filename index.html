Sara Tosheva

My story begins on September 26, 2004 in Kocani. In a small but beautiful rural area in the eastern region of our country - the village of Istibanja, where I started my primary education. Then, I continued my education in high school as an economic technician in Kocani. A passion for traveling and discovering new places fueled my desire to pursue my "Dream job" – a job that would allow me to work remotely and travel constantly. I currently live in Skopje, study and work to fulfill my desire.

The sentence that guides me through life is: "After the rain comes the rainbow."

In my free time I like to read books and I love to get lost in the worlds I discover through reading. I have been a member of a folk ensemble for more than a decade now, and I really enjoy it – it fulfills me. In the last two years, my hobby turned into a job - I became an assistant in the ensemble. I spend the most precious time with my family and friends.
